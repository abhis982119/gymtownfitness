Common Questions

Can I freeze my membership?

Growth and Pro plans include freeze options. Contact us to pause your membership for the allowed period.

Is there a joining fee?

No joining fee at all. The price you see is exactly what you pay — fully transparent with nothing hidden.

Can I upgrade my plan?

Yes. Upgrade at any time and we will prorate the remaining value of your current plan towards the new one.

Student discounts?

Students with a valid ID receive 15% off any plan. Contact us and we will apply the discount to your membership.
